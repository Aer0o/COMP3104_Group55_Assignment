####### ABOUT THE IMPORTANCE OF SDLC AND DEVOPS ######


#---Section 1: What is DevOps in the SDLC?---#

DevOps is a methodology that integrates software development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops) to improve collaboration, automate workflows, and accelerate the software development life cycle (SDLC). It bridges the gap between teams, enabling more efficient and reliable software delivery.



#---Section 2: Why DevOps is Important in the SDLC---#

Learning DevOps is crucial because it helps streamline processes in the SDLC, such as development, testing, deployment, and monitoring. By adopting DevOps practices, teams can reduce errors, shorten development cycles, and respond faster to customer feedback.
